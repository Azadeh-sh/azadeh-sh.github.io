Project information

  • Subject: Developer Panel Dashboard Redesign
  • My Role: UX Research/Design, Visual and UI Design
  • Client: Myket Appstore
  • Project date: jun 2021
  • Project URL: https://developer.myket.ir/

Overview

Myket is an android appstore and developers publish their apps on it. As for appstore one of the most important part is developers who publish their apps on it. Myket support team was busy with developers calls.So for the first step they asked for finding the problem and reduce the calls.



Gathering Data

I gathered some information about developers from google analyics, database and data analysists



Competitive Analysis

I researched similar 5 AppStores in the market. It allowed me to identify their Strengths and opportunities for my project too.





Talking to Support team and interview developers

To understand the case better, I talked to the support team who had a direct connection with developers, and also interviewd with different developers. By doing this I captured how they use the dashboard too and what value they took from it and what actions they looked for.





Persona

Based on the research I created user personas to create clean picture of developer's goals, needs and behaviour.





I listed some of their problems:

  • They want to see their apps insights quickly
  • they spend time to find frequent actions
  • they have different experience of publishing app in other similar panels and this is different
  • they can't find easily online support, guides ,and knowledgebase for publishing or writing their apps


HMW

I made HMW questions to talk to support team and our developer team about the problem and find better ideas

  • How might we help developers to publish their apps on myket easily?
  • How might we reduce clicks to reach developers actions?
  • How might we can support usrs online that they don't need calling support team?
  • How might we aware them from their apps insight?


Card Sorting

I did closed cardsorting to found out what should be shown on dashboard. I made three categories:"frequently used","sometime used" and "never used" then I asked developers to put their actions in each group





WireFrame

Based on the information I Sketched Wireframes and show them to support team and five developers to gather their ideas before prototype



Protorype

I asked some of developers to test the prototype. I asked them some tasks such as add new program or tell me their apps sale to capture their actions, also asked them about their likes or dislikes.

By testing the prototype I noticed that there wasn't a consistency between their application (that developers apps published on) and their dashboard. I reached to:

  • structured and consistent navigation
  • Similar icons and colors


IconGraphy And Color

I designed icons and colors in the way to be consistent with the appstore that developers to felt the familier environment
